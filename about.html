About Us

We are a boutique family-run classic car rental service operating out of Notting Hill.

A father-son team who love classic vehicles.

From classic Minis, to series 1 Land Rover’s, split-screen campervans, old BMWs and Mercedes, 90’s French hatchbacks, and much more besides - we have enjoyed many classic cars together.

Stemming from a love of classic cars, we have sought to create a business around this passion. One which brings people with shared interests together, and which can support those who continue to maintain beautiful and interesting machines.

Our Ethos

We recognise the difficulties of owning a classic car. The nightmarish splutter on a cold morning. The nerve wrecking rattle or creak on the motorway. Classic cars can be anxiety inducing. But they can also be pure joy.

Our aim is to bring this joy of classic cars to more people, not just those with the flexibility to own them.

This is where our emphasis on weekend, or longer, rentals comes from. We believe that you need more than a few hours to really form an emotional connection with a machine. This is why we keep our prices as low as possible.

Mutually Beneficial Approach

We aim to provide classic car owners and restorers with a means to generate revenue through their machines.

This, we hope, will enable individuals and businesses to continue restoring and maintaining classic cars.

We want to also encourage people to purchase older, more interesting vehicles.

Sustainability

When manufacturing and shipping is taken into consideration, the production of new cars is an extremely costly process to our planet. Not only does maintaining classic cars lead to more enlivened roads, but it is also a form of recycling.

Continuing to use the vehicles that have already been manufactured is a key way to reduce global emissions.

We are committed to contributing toward a green future. If you are interested in ULEZ compliance or electric conversions, please do get in contact.

Client Loyalty

We want to incentivise our clients to spend as much time as possible with classic cars. This is why we keep our prices low.

We don’t go in for hourly rentals as we believe more time is needed to form a proper attachment to a vehicle.

New opportunities and suggestions are always cropping up for our clients. The benefits are huge. See where becoming our client can take you by enquiring below.
